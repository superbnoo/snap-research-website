Research Fellowship

The Snap Research Fellowship program is designed to foster a strong collaboration between Snap Research and the brightest, most driven graduate computer science students across the world. We aim to provide students additional resources and freedom to pursue innovative ideas, resulting in cutting-edge research and products. Snap Research Fellows receive a $10,000 academic award.
